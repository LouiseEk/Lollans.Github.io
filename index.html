Numbers and facts about the Swedish fashion and textile industry









In the year of 2014, the turnover of the Swedish fashion industry was 264 billion SEK in total. The export vs. sales on the Swedish market was there by 65 % vs. 35%, which equals 173 billion SEK and SEK.



















The fashion and textile industris is overall a women dominated industri with 80% women as employees and 20% men.

The CEO positions tho, are mostly held by men, with the numbers of 70% men and 30% women. "Why the hell is that", one might wonder?









Source: The Swedish Textile & Fashion company https://www.teko.se/
